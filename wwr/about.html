History

Rafting Travel started floating the river in 1995 when a group of friends turned their passion into a business. Our commitment has always been simple: share the thrill of whitewater rafting while preserving the beauty of nature. From humble beginnings offering local trips, we have grown into a leading provider of comprehensive rafting expeditions worldwide.
